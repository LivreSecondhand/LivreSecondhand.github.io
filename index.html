About Us

A secondhand book from Livre is more than just a preloved item, it’s a cherished piece of history, a treasure trove of memorable tales, and a testament to the joy of reading. Each book in our collection carries a legacy of great stories and moments, waiting to be rediscovered and loved all over again. Remember “Story is Mandatory”.

By choosing a secondhand book from Livre, you're not just acquiring a reading companion, but also contributing to a more sustainable future. You're giving a cherished book a second life, reducing waste, and supporting a circular economy. Discover the joy of sustainable reading and embrace the unique charm of a book that has been loved before.
